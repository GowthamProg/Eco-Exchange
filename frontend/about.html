Why We Exist

Every year, India generates over 3.2 million tonnes of e-waste, and less than 20% is formally recycled. Most ends up in landfills or is mishandled in informal sectors, leading to environmental pollution, health hazards, and loss of valuable materials.

We created E-Waste Exchange as a service-based digital marketplace to solve this problem by:

  • Giving people an easy and secure way to dispose of or sell their old electronics
  • Encouraging repair over replace, saving both money and materials
  • Incentivizing proper disposal with a token-to-cash reward system
  • Building a trust-based connection between buyers, sellers, and recyclers

Backed by Research

According to a joint report by UNEP, NITI Aayog, and the International Telecommunication Union, e-waste contains precious metals like gold, silver, and rare earth elements.

The improper disposal of e-waste is not only an environmental disaster but also a huge missed economic opportunity.

This platform is designed to align with the UN's Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 12 - Responsible Consumption and Production), promoting a circular economy by facilitating reuse, repair, and recycle.
